About BDC

A homegrown firm built for Sarawak's next chapter.

Borneo Development Capital is a Malaysia-based private equity firm focused exclusively on the long-term development of Sarawak. We deploy patient capital across infrastructure, public-market equity, and high-growth digital sectors aligned with the state's 2030 development agenda.

Our team combines institutional capital markets experience from Hong Kong and Malaysia with deep, on-the-ground operational knowledge of Sarawak. We invest with one conviction: that the next decade of Borneo's growth deserves capital that understands the place.

Investment Approach

Three strategies, balanced across the risk-return spectrum.

We construct portfolios that pair the stability of contractual cashflow with the upside of structured equity and high-growth opportunities - designed for capital preservation first, returns second.
01 - Core Yield

Government & Infrastructure Financing

Defensive · Contractual cashflow

Yield-focused investments alongside state and government-linked project contracts, providing predictable income and strong downside protection.

02 - Structured Equity

Listed Company Participation

Balanced · Yield with upside

Selective participation in public-market opportunities including IPO and pre-IPO situations, with risk mitigation and disciplined position-sizing built into each transaction.

03 - Future Economy

High-Growth & Digital Infrastructure

Growth · Strategic alignment

Selective exposure to scalable platforms in digital infrastructure, data centres, and the future-economy sectors aligned with Sarawak's long-term agenda.

The Thesis

From Watt to Byte. Sarawak builds the next economy.

Abundant clean energy, stable climate, and vast underused land - Sarawak's natural endowment is being converted into the foundation of a digital economy.

The state's development blueprint targets a doubling of GDP to RM282 billion by 2030, anchored by renewable generation, data infrastructure, and the structural advantages that few jurisdictions in Southeast Asia possess at scale.
