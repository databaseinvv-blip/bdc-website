Privacy Policy

How Borneo Development Capital collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data, in accordance with Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010.

Last updated: May 2026
Note to readers: Borneo Development Capital takes the protection of personal data seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the rights you have under Malaysian law.

1. About this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Borneo Development Capital PLT ("BDC", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal data in the course of operating its website at borneodevcapital.com and conducting its business activities.

This policy is issued in accordance with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 ("PDPA") and applies to all personal data processed by BDC in respect of commercial transactions.

2. Personal data we collect

We may collect personal data through this website and through our broader business activities, including:

Information you provide directly

  • Contact information: name, email address, telephone number, and postal address provided when you contact us through the website, email, or other channels;
  • Professional information: employer, job title, professional credentials, and similar information you choose to share with us;
  • Investor information: if you are a Sophisticated Investor or prospective investor, additional information required for investor onboarding, including identification, financial qualification, and beneficial ownership details, collected through separate investor onboarding procedures and not through this public website;
  • Career-related information: CVs, employment history, and supporting documents submitted in connection with career enquiries;
  • Correspondence: content of emails, letters, or other communications you send to us.

Information collected automatically

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, and referring website;
  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent on the website, and general interaction patterns;
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see section 7 below.

3. How we use personal data

We use personal data for the following purposes:

  • Responding to enquiries received through the website or other channels;
  • Processing career applications and considering candidates for available roles;
  • Conducting investor onboarding, where applicable, in compliance with anti-money laundering, counter-financing of terrorism, and know-your-client requirements;
  • Communicating with investors and prospective investors about fund-related matters, where consent has been obtained;
  • Operating, maintaining, and improving the website;
  • Complying with legal, regulatory, and reporting obligations under Malaysian law;
  • Protecting against fraud, unauthorised access, and other security risks.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process personal data on the following legal bases under the PDPA:

  • Consent: where you have provided consent for specific processing activities;
  • Contractual necessity: where processing is necessary to enter into or perform a contract with you;
  • Legal obligation: where processing is required to comply with Malaysian law or regulatory requirements;
  • Legitimate interests: where processing is necessary for our legitimate interests in operating the business, provided these interests are not overridden by your rights.

5. Disclosure of personal data

We may disclose personal data to:

  • Service providers: third parties who provide services to BDC, including IT, hosting (Cloudflare), email, and professional advisors (legal, accounting, audit). These parties are required to handle personal data in accordance with applicable laws and our instructions;
  • Regulators: the Securities Commission Malaysia, the Inland Revenue Board, or other regulatory or governmental authorities, where required by law or in connection with our regulatory obligations;
  • Group entities and partners: affiliated entities and co-investment partners, where necessary for the conduct of investment activities;
  • Professional advisors: external lawyers, auditors, and consultants engaged by BDC;
  • In connection with corporate transactions: if BDC is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Cross-border data transfers

Some of our service providers may be located outside Malaysia, including Cloudflare (which provides the hosting and content delivery infrastructure for this website). Where personal data is transferred outside Malaysia, we take reasonable steps to ensure an adequate level of protection is in place, in accordance with the requirements of the PDPA.

7. Cookies and similar technologies

This website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Operate essential website functions;
  • Improve security and prevent abuse, including through Cloudflare's protection services;
  • Understand general usage patterns through anonymised analytics, where applicable.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the website.

8. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including any legal, regulatory, accounting, or reporting requirements. Specifically:

  • General enquiry data: retained for as long as needed to respond to your enquiry, and typically for up to 24 months thereafter unless a relationship is established;
  • Investor records: retained for the duration of the investor relationship and for the period required by applicable laws and regulations following the end of the relationship;
  • Career application data: retained for up to 12 months after the application is reviewed unless consent is obtained for longer retention.

After the applicable retention period, we will securely delete or anonymise the personal data.

9. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include access controls, encryption where appropriate, and secure data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your rights under the PDPA

Under the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010, you have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct personal data that is inaccurate or incomplete;
  • Withdraw consent for processing where consent was previously provided;
  • Limit processing of personal data for direct marketing purposes;
  • Lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commissioner of Malaysia.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in section 12.

11. Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children, and BDC does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a minor without verified parental consent, we will take steps to delete that data.
